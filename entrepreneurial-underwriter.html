Event Update

MGA Rendezvous Registrations Set Rapid Pace

More than 170 delegates have so far registered to attend the 2026 MGA Rendezvous, representing 35 MGAs, 20 carriers and seven service providers. Total attendance at the conference, which is due to take place at the Hotel Arts in Barcelona on May 11 and 12, is now expected to exceed 500.

The conference will be co-hosted this year by FASE and the Spanish MGA association, ASASE and sponsored by Howden. It will feature presentations and panel discussions with business leaders from Europe and the United States, but a significant portion of the day and a half event will be given over to networking.

FASE members are entitled to a 50% discount on passes. For an MGA, a single conference pass would therefore cost €400; for a carrier or broker, €550; and for a service provider €700.
