**FASE (Fédération des Agences de Souscription Européennes) is delighted to welcome Accelerant as a Silver sponsor of the federation.**

&nbsp;

Accelerant began its journey in Europe in 2018, before expanding to the United States in 2020. The Accelerant Risk Exchange now supports more than 265 MGAs globally.

&nbsp;

Christopher Lee-Smith, co-founder and head of distribution at Accelerant, said: "The establishment of FASE marks an important milestone in the development of the European MGA market, providing a single voice and forum for the market. We are delighted to support it."

&nbsp;

FASE executive director William Pitt said: "The Accelerant Risk Exchange provides sustained underwriting capacity and critical business intelligence to MGAs across Europe. Both are essential for the European MGA market to realize its considerable potential."
