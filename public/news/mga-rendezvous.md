# The MGA Rendezvous | Hotel Arts, Barcelona, 11-12 May 2026 | Where Europe welcomes the world

FASE (Fédération des Agences de Souscription Européennes) is proud to partner with ASASE to co-host the inaugural MGA Rendezvous at the Hotel Arts Barcelona on May 11 - 12, 2026.

Sponsored by Howden, the MGA Rendezvous will provide a unique forum for the MGA community to connect, collaborate, share insight and create new business opportunities across the full spectrum of the delegated authority ecosystem.

## Key Features

- **The first truly pan-European forum** bringing together MGAs, brokers, insurers and reinsurers
- **A meeting place for a €20 billion market** growing at more than 20% annually, reflecting the rapid expansion and innovation of the European MGA sector
- **A world-class programme** of presentations, speakers and panel discussions by leading experts from the European and global MGA markets
- **Structured networking** designed for efficiency and impact, with participants sharing information on their risk appetite, expertise, and areas of focus ahead of the event to maximise the value of every meeting
