**FASE is delighted to welcome Accredited - Specialty Insurer as a Platinum sponsor of the federation.**

&nbsp;

"We are excited to partner with FASE to support the continuing development of a dynamic and highly professional MGA market in Europe," said Stuart McMurdo, CEO, Accredited Insurance Limited, Europe & UK. "Knowledge sharing around best practices will be crucial in enabling the market to realize its enormous potential. FASE can help and has our wholehearted support."

&nbsp;

Accredited has a long history of engagement with the European MGA market. Founded in 2013, Accredited Insurance (Europe) Ltd provides insurance capacity to MGAs across the UK and continental Europe. With a financial strength rating of A- from A.M. Best, the company is authorized to write all non-life classes of insurance throughout the European Economic Area. Through its UK branch, it also offers a Brexit solution to MGAs writing business in the UK and continental Europe.

&nbsp;

William Pitt, executive director of FASE, said: "Expertise founded on deep experience is always at a premium in insurance. We look forward to sharing the benefits of Accredited's rich experience in the delegated authority market with our members."
