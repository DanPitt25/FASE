**Loro is a no-code insurtech platform that enables insurance companies, managing general agents and brokers to distribute and manage insurance policies. It aims to bring new products to market in days instead of months, significantly accelerating time-to-premium.**

&nbsp;

"We at Loro are very happy to support FASE in its mission to provide a forum and a voice for MGAs across Europe," said Peter Tilbrook, Loro CEO. "MGAs are raising the tempo of new product development in European insurance, contributing to the development of a more innovative and responsive market. Our platform has been built to support this vibrant and fast-growing sector of the specialty insurance market."

&nbsp;

William Pitt, executive director at FASE, said: "Technology tailored to the needs of MGAs will play a key role in driving the growth of the European MGA market. We are delighted to welcome Loro as a founder member and sponsor of FASE."
